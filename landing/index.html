Engineering Judgement

Why these challenges deserve repeated work

Reliability is the entry ticket

In the physical world, 95% accuracy is not deployment-grade. A single false trigger can damage equipment or create safety incidents.

Reproducibility makes discussion meaningful

If results only work on one "lucky machine," that is coincidence. Cross-device, cross-region reproduction is real engineering evidence.

Boundaries matter more than capability

The stronger the agent, the clearer the boundaries: which actions are autonomous, which need human confirmation, which must be denied.

We don't bet on immediate deployment. We bet on one more verifiable piece of evidence every week.
